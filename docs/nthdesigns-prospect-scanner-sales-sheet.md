## The problem

UK local businesses lose enquiries in two invisible ways:

1. **Google Business Profile neglect** — weak reviews, missing photos, incomplete listings while competitors capture Maps visibility worth **£3–25 per paid click**.
2. **Website accessibility failures** — WCAG violations that block bookings, with **EAA/EHRC enforcement** now live for professional services firms.

Agencies and freelancers waste hours manually prospecting, auditing one site at a time, and writing cold emails that prospects dismiss as generic sales noise.

---

## The solution

**nthdesigns Prospect Scanner** finds weak local businesses, scores them on GBP + accessibility, generates a **shareable proof report**, and drafts **personalised outreach** — in one workflow.

> *Find → Score → Prove → Convert*

---

## How it works (60 seconds)

| Step | What happens |
|------|----------------|
| **1. Discover** | Run a niche + city search (e.g. “private dentist, Birmingham”) or audit a single URL |
| **2. Score** | Dual-signal weakness ranking: GBP gaps vs local #1 competitor + axe-core/Lighthouse violations |
| **3. Prove** | Auto-generate an unlisted report (`/r/{token}`) — grade, violations, screenshots, competitor comparison |
| **4. Convert** | AI drafts outreach with the right pitch angle; embed report link; prospect books via report CTA |
